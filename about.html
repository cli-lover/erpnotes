About Me & This Blog

Learn more about the author and the purpose behind this Odoo learning blog.

About the Author

Hi! I'm Apeiron, a passionate developer who has been exploring Odoo ERP and its ecosystem. This blog is my way of documenting everything I learn along the way, from the basic concepts of Odoo to more advanced topics like building custom modules and integrating third-party apps.

Why I Started This Blog

While learning Odoo, I found that documenting my notes helped me understand the concepts better and allowed me to reference back to them easily. This blog was born out of that need. I hope it also helps others who are on their journey to mastering Odoo, whether you're a beginner or looking to dive deeper into its functionality.

My goal is to break down complex concepts into easy-to-understand language and provide clear examples. I focus on explaining how Odoo works at a fundamental level while also showing how you can extend and customize it for various business needs.

What to Expect

On this blog, you can expect to find:

  • Step-by-step tutorials on building Odoo modules.
  • Deep dives into the Odoo framework and architecture.
  • Practical examples and coding tips.
  • Notes from my personal experience learning Odoo.

Thank you for visiting! I hope you find the content helpful in your Odoo journey. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or suggestions.
